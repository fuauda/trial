About us

BICAP Recap website is designed to help high school students to recap key points in different subjects. A recap allows students to revisit key concepts and information covered in the subject, reinforcing their understanding and retention of the material. A recap provides an opportunity for students to ask questions and seek clarification on any topic they may have found confusing or challenging. A recap also helps students review thier knowledge before exams, insuring they are well prepared to demonistrate their understanding of the material. Overall, a subject recap plays a crucial role in enhancing student learning outcomes and overall academic performance.
To reinforce learning, clarify doubts, prepare for exams, reflection and self assessiment, we will keep on uploading recap of other subjects such as chemistry, physics, astronomy and IT.
